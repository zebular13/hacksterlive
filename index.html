Welcome to Hackster Live's home on Github.

In 2016 we set Hackster on a new mission: reach 50,000 makers and developers around the world through a physical network of Meetups and Workshops, focused on IoT education in the 21st century. Led by a network of passionate ambassadors, we're giving our community and partners a voice in envisioning what's next in hardware, the cloud, and connectivity, and the magic that happens when they all come together.
